Know Our People and Activities


The area is largely populated by OBC communities, with a small SC population and a very small ST population.

Most families work in informal sectors such as factory labor, ragpicking, unorganized street-side businesses, and daily wage jobs. Recent changes in labor laws and longer working hours have further strained their living conditions.

Average household income: ₹10,000–₹25,000 per month Average number of children per family: 3–4 These socio-economic challenges have a long-term adverse impact on children's futures, leading to increased vulnerability to drug abuse, illegal or criminal activities, child labor, and early marriages.

Educational Landscape: 1. 15 Private Schools (offering both International and National syllabi) and 12 Low-Fee Private Schools.
2. 6 Government Schools (up to 12th Grade, operating under a Single Teacher Model, adopted by foundations.)
3. 24 Premium Private Tuition Centers (charging ₹800₹3000 per subject, per hour)
4. 46+ Home Tuition Providers usually one teacher handling all subjects and languages for students from Grade 1 to Grade 9
