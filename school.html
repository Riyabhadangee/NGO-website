Micro School - Launching Soon

Launching Growth Platter Academy (An Initiative by Lamp Educational and Charitable Trust). A school for every students to thrive in a complex world by making learning hands-on, purposeful, and deeply rooted in real world challenges.

NIOS Open Schooling to empowers learners to chart their own educational journey. With access to a full range of NIOS subjects, students can craft a personalized learning experience from primary to high school—one that blends academic depth with hands-on, project-based learning. The result: a flexible, meaningful, and future ready approach to education.

Crafting Portfolios. Mapping Futures. At Growth Platter Academy, learners build personalized portfolios that showcase their talents, creativity, and progress. Each project is guided by a dedicated career advisor who connects students with mentors, college pathways, and real-world opportunities turning aspirations into action.

Flexible Subjects:- At Growth Platter Academy, your child isn’t limited to rigid subject lists. They can combine subjects that match their passions and strengths from science and art to psychology and business. Many of these pathways are portfolio-assessed, helping reduce test anxiety and encouraging greater ownership of the learning process.

Advanced Learning Spaces, Empowered Students From AI-assisted learning to live e-portfolios, Growth Platter Academy uses cutting-edge technology to equip learners with tomorrow’s tools while keeping parents engaged through real-time app updates and progress dashboards
