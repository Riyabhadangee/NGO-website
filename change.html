Changemakers Fellowship

At Lamp Educational and Charitable Trust, we believe that real change begins at the community level, driven by passionate individuals who are willing to take action. The Changemakers Fellowship is an upcoming initiative designed to empower young leaders with the knowledge, tools, and mentorship needed to design and lead social impact projects. Fellows will be guided by experts in design thinking, civic innovation, and community engagement, enabling them to identify pressing local issues and create sustainable, people-centered solutions.

The program offers hands-on learning through interactive workshops, mentorship from industry professionals, and peer collaboration, giving participants the chance to transform ideas into action. From conceptualizing a project to implementing it in their own community, fellows will gain real-world experience in problem-solving, leadership, and impact measurement.

By the end of the fellowship, participants will not only have led a meaningful community initiative but will also emerge as confident changemakers equipped with the skills to inspire others and drive long-term transformation. Through this fellowship, Lamp Educational and Charitable Trust envisions building a strong network of grassroots leaders who are committed to creating inclusive, innovative, and sustainable solutions for society.
