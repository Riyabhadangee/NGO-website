Students Entrepreneur Founder’s Club

At Lamp Educational and Charitable Trust, we believe that innovation and leadership begin at a young age. To nurture this spirit, we are launching the Students Entrepreneur Founder’s Club, an exclusive platform for students who aspire to become entrepreneurs or solopreneurs. This program is designed to take students through the complete journey of ideation to implementation, helping them turn creative concepts into real-world ventures.

Through regular club sessions, participants will gain exposure to entrepreneurial thinking, problem-solving skills, and market research techniques. They will learn how to design and test business ideas, create prototypes, and develop business models that are both sustainable and impactful. With guidance from mentors and industry experts, students will also practice building investor-ready pitches, gaining the confidence to present their ideas to potential funders and collaborators.

The club will provide a collaborative environment where young minds can share ideas, work on projects, and learn from each other’s experiences. By engaging in workshops, hackathons, and networking opportunities, students will not only develop entrepreneurial skills but also build the resilience and leadership qualities required to thrive as independent founders.

By the end of the program, members of the Students Entrepreneur Founder’s Club will be empowered with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to take their first steps as changemakers in the entrepreneurial ecosystem. At Lamp Educational and Charitable Trust, we see this club as a catalyst to transform bright ideas into ventures that create lasting social and economic impact.
