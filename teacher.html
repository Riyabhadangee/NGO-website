Teacher Training Program

At Lamp Educational and Charitable Trust, we recognize that teachers are the backbone of a strong and inclusive education system. To support the next generation of educators, we are launching our Teacher Training Program, specially designed for aspiring teachers who wish to make a meaningful impact in the classroom. This program will provide hands-on experience in modern teaching methodologies, equipping participants with practical skills to engage learners effectively. Special focus will be given to inclusive education practices, ensuring that teachers can cater to the diverse needs of students across different learning levels and backgrounds.

Through interactive sessions, workshops, and classroom simulations, participants will explore innovative techniques such as project-based learning, activity-driven pedagogy, and digital tools for education. They will also learn strategies to create safe, inclusive, and motivating classroom environments where every student can thrive. Guided by experienced educators and mentors, trainees will not only gain theoretical knowledge but also develop the confidence to apply these methods in real classroom settings.
