Entrepreneurship Program

At Lamp Educational and Charitable Trust, we believe that true empowerment begins with knowledge, opportunity, and the confidence to create change. In line with this vision, we are introducing our upcoming Entrepreneurship Program, designed to inspire and equip aspiring entrepreneurs with the tools to transform their ideas into impactful ventures. The program will take participants step by step through the entrepreneurial journey—starting from developing an innovative idea, building a sustainable business model, understanding customer needs, and crafting a clear go-to-market strategy.

Through practical workshops and guided mentorship, participants will learn how to prepare professional pitch presentations and approach funders with confidence. They will also gain insights into financial literacy, resource management, and the importance of social responsibility in business. Real-world case studies, success stories, and interactive sessions with industry experts will provide them with a strong foundation for navigating challenges and seizing opportunities.

By the end of the program, participants will not only have the confidence to launch their ventures but will also develop the entrepreneurial mindset of resilience, problem-solving, and innovation. At Lamp Educational and Charitable Trust, we envision this program as more than just a training initiative—it is a pathway for young minds to become job creators, community builders, and leaders of tomorrow
