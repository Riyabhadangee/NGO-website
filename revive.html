Environmental Sustainability Awareness Program Project Revive Earth - Launching Soon! 1st September 2025

At Lamp Educational and Charitable Trust, we believe education is not complete without nurturing responsibility towards our planet. That’s why we are proud to launch Revive Earth — an initiative that empowers children to become champions of environmental sustainability.

Through this program, we create awareness among our students about the urgent need to protect our environment from landfills, burning of waste, and pollution of water and soil. Children learn not only about the science behind climate change but also about the social and climate impacts that affect our daily lives and communities.

Our purpose is simple yet powerful — to see a clean future starting from our own homes, then moving to the next house, one step at a time. “One Child. One Home. One Clean Earth.” By instilling these values early, we aim to raise a generation of eco-conscious citizens who can drive positive change for our society and the planet.

With Revive Earth, children don’t just learn why sustainability matters, but also how they can take action — through waste reduction, recycling, repurposing, and everyday choices that protect nature.

Because when children lead the way, the earth revives with hope
